Layout Principles
  • Space is the primary design element
  • 80–120px+ vertical section spacing
  • 500–700px max text width, centered
  • One idea per section
  • Espresso-led backgrounds, selective contrast
  • Mobile-first, 44px min tap targets
  • Line height 1.5–1.8, generous breathing
  • No bold above Medium/500, no all-caps

Imagery & Visual Language
Use
  • Light entering a space (windows, doorways, canopies)
  • Reflections (water, glass, mirrors)
  • Sky, horizon, natural textures (linen, sand, stone, wood)
  • Warm hearth / firelight
  • Subtle geometric / mandala patterns
  • Welcome Home mat as core motif
Avoid
  • Cliché spiritual (lotus, third eyes, chakras)
  • Overly literal visuals
  • Busy compositions
  • Staged / corporate stock photos
  • Heavy filters or dark moody aesthetics
Treatment: Soft natural color grading · Warm tone overlay · Contemplative compositions · Images should feel found, not produced.
Accessibility — “Care as Cause”
Ivory on Espresso = primary high-contrast treatment. CTA buttons: Espresso text on Amber bg. Glow and accent enhance atmosphere but never reduce readability. Non-negotiable.
What This Is Not
  • Not a spiritual authority platform
  • Not promotional or conversion-driven
  • Not ornate, busy, or loud
  • Not instructional or dogmatic
  • Not a traditional website — it’s a living space
  • Guiding feeling: “Returning home to something already known”
