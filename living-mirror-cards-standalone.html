Living Mirror Cards

Portable companions for self-inquiry. Print them, carry them, put one on your actual mirror. Each card is an invitation — not an instruction.

Tip: Use "Save as PDF" in the print dialog to download a digital copy.

Inquiry Pathway Cards
Inquiry Pathway

What Do I Value?

Not what you've been told. What actually makes you feel light.
Reflection Questions
  • When everything feels right — not productive, just right — what is present?
  • What have I been carrying that isn't mine?
  • When was I most myself — not performing, not proving?
  • If no one would ever know, what would I choose?
  • What am I willing to protect, even when it costs me?

Inquiry Pathway

What Is My Intention?

Beneath the plans — what is the deeper movement?
Reflection Questions
  • What is the feeling I'm actually reaching for?
  • Am I trying to prove something through what I'm building?
  • If I did nothing — what would remain on its own?
  • Does this make me feel light or heavy? In the body.
  • "What I really want is to..." — finish without editing.
The Living Mirror · houseoflightandlove.org
Inquiry Pathway

How Do I Navigate This?

The disturbance is a road sign, not a wall.
Reflection Questions
  • Name the disturbance plainly — without the story.
  • Where do I feel this in my body right now?
  • What old pattern is this activating?
  • If this were a messenger, not a punishment — what is it delivering?
  • What would my trusting self do — not my perfect self?
