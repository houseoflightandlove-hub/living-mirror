The Invitation

A door that is always open

House of Light and Love is an open community — a quiet, invitational space for anyone who wants to pause, reflect, and remember what already lives as love. There is no membership, no doctrine, no hierarchy, and no agenda beyond presence, honesty, and care.

The door is always open. All are always welcome.

The Vision

Open doorways toward…

House of Light and Love is not an organization — it is an open community. A living space where anyone can walk in, sit down, and be met exactly as they are. No application, no requirements, no conversion. Just presence.

Every visitor crosses the same threshold: Welcome Home. Not welcome to a program. Not welcome to a belief system. Welcome home to yourself — to what was never missing, only forgotten.

The House exists to hold space for that remembrance. Nothing more. Nothing less.

"Love is not separate from us. It is what we are, and all that is."

The Foundation

The Brick of Love

The House is built by everyone who enters.

  • Each person places a handprint with their name, a message of love, and where they are from
  • The Foundation grows endlessly — visible, living proof of a community connected by love
  • If inspired, your contribution helps sustain the House and its offerings to the world
  • The brick is free. The donation is gratitude. Nothing is required.

A love offering — not a membership fee. The Foundation belongs to everyone who places a brick, and every brick makes the House stronger.
